Education

Sociology, Panteion University of Athens

Completed successfully with 7.4/10.0
During the above study programme, I have studied the origins and importance of sociology as well as the most important sociologists, their work and associated research. Examples in this area include Karl Marx and Max Weber. Emphasis was given to various important methods and techniques for examining various aspects and modern issues societies face. Special focus was given on the statistical methods needed to better understand and analyse data and patterns to answer some of the key social-oriented questions that can help society function more effectively. We have also analysed the theory behind social layering and foundations, also applied to modern societies. Introduction was given to crime and its strong connection to sociology. Examination of the factors affecting this relationship was performed. This is an area that fascinated me and I decided to take more courses to enhance my knowledge. Overall, completing my degree, I felt that I obtained a deeper understanding of society's foundation and its origins, of various issues modern societies face and the various methods used to analyse and attempt to solve these.
